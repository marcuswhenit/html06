Who Is tana?

Amari Middleton (born May 20, 2003), professionally known as OsamaSon, is a recording artist and record producer from Goose Creek, South Carolina.
OsamaSon started rapping in mid-2020, releasing his first project, Im Da Man, in August of 2021. After had releasing a slew of projects throughout 2021 and 2022, exclusively through SoundCloud, OsamaSon would see his first success in the underground scene in April 2023, with the song “cts-v” He would see similar success with his follow-up single, “Thuggin W My Rounds” the same month.
On July 21, 2023, OsamaSon would release his debut studio album, Osama Season, which would earn him immediate success, thanks to songs such as "Werkin", “Kutta,” “Lil O,” and most notably “X & Sex,” getting attention through social media. He would earn himself a record deal with Atlantic Records around the time Osama Season released.
Since the release of Osama Season, OsamaSon has since continued to release music, with his follow up single “Trenches,” capturing the same success as his previous singles. OsamaSon has also been seen collaborating with other underground artists, such as Nettspend and Slump6s.
On December 8, 2023, OsamaSon released his highly anticipated sophomore mixtape, Flex Musix. OsamaSon would then enter the year 2024 with the release of FLXTRA, the official deluxe to Flex Musix, on February 16.
