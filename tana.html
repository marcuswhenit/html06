Who Is tana?

Steven Lewis (born November 14, 2006), professionally known as tana, is a recording artist and record producer from Columbus, Georgia.
tana started sharing his "musical creations" in 2018 on YouTube and SoundCloud, releasing his first song, Red Eye!, in 2020. After releasing a bunch of singlesthrough 2020, tana would see his first success in the underground scene in late 2020, with the song “Prada”.
In 2021, tana would release his the song that gained him a log of attention in the music world, Antisocial (feat. Slump6s), which would earn him attention through TikTok and other record labels. tana was also tied to other underground artists such as KA$HDAMI, midwxst, yvngxchris, and others. They would occasionally feature eachother on songs so they could all gain recognition.
Since the release of Anticocial, tana has since continued to release music, with his follow up single “Antisocial 2” making a sequel to his most popular song, and featuring more artists like Slump6s, Yung Fazo, SSGKobe, and Xhulooo.
On December 8, 2023, tana released his first studio album, Gaultier. tana would then enter the year 2024 with the release of "Decatur" and "Jesus Piece", these songs are part of an anticipated album labeled "Bana". tana has been teasing the album since November of 2023 with plans to near the 3rd or 4th quarter of 2024.
