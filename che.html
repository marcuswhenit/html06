Who Is che?

Chase Shaun Mitchell (born August 29, 2006), professionally known as OsamaSon, is a recording artist and record producer from Atlanta, Georgia.
che started rapping in 2021, releasing his first song, kind, in september of 2021. After che had released a couple singles through SoundCloud and spotify, che would see his first success in the underground scene in April 2023, with the song “agenda”. The song spread around TikTok like a wildfire making him one of the most popular ungerground artists at the time
On Feburary 10th, 2023, che would release some teaser songs for his next album "closed captions". He released 2 songs with the named of blac chyna, and bluberry bakwood. che released his album on July 21st, 2023 which would earn him immediate success, thanks to songs such as "sol", “frank ocean,” “flat (bonus),” and most notably “blac chyna". Unlike OsamaSon, che had his fair share of social media exposure already and was already talked about, so the album stayed pretty closed off to a niche audience.
Since the release of closed captions, che has since continued to release music. On October 11th, 2023, che released a small project called "Before Crueger" that only had 2 songs. everyone knew that he was going to drop another album or EP soon because "Before Ccrueger" states that this was literally before a project named Crueger. che released his EP, Crueger on October 31st, 2023."
For 2024, che previed a snippet of a song labled "Bae" on TikTok. This video gained lots of traction and had people wanting che to release the full song to the public. With it,s popularity, che decided to drop the song on Feburary 14th, 2024.
